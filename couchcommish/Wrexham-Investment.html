Visionaries on the Pitch: The Story of Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney's Bold Wrexham Investment

Brandon Novarro | May 7, 2023 • 4 min

Wrexham Association Football Club, the third-oldest club in the world, was once in dire straits. Struggling financially and on the pitch, the club was at risk of falling into obscurity. However, the fortunes of the club took a dramatic turn when Hollywood actors Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney stepped in with a bold and unconventional investment plan.

For some, purchasing and owning your very own sports team is a life-long dream. While unconventional, Reynolds and McElhenney, decided to do just this in November of 2020, purchasing the 5th league Welsh football club, Wrexham AFC. While some manage a midlife crisis by purchasing a sports car, the two famous actors decided to buy a sports team, but not any team, a lower-league Welsh football club.

It’s hard to imagine how successful an investment in a team that was just one relegation from being in a non-professional football league would be. However, Rob and Ryan’s early tenure as owners shows their genius strategy in creating a winner, both on the pitch and for their pockets. Both successful actors and entrepreneurs understood the importance of creative storytelling and marketing genius. They spent countless hours meeting with fans and supporters, building relationships, and taking in feedback. Their $2.5 million investment in the club's players and facilities, coupled with a 25-year stadium lease, was a game-changer for Wrexham FC and its supporters.

Rob and Ryan celebrating the recent promotion of Wrexham AFC

The launch of the docuseries "Welcome to Wrexham" was a key part of Reynolds and McElhenney's investment strategy. The series offered a behind-the-scenes look at the club's journey from the brink of obscurity to global stardom. With its engaging storytelling and marketing genius, the series generated $3.2 million in revenue and received rave reviews from audiences.

Reynolds and McElhenney's shrewd business acumen and visionary approach were instrumental in the success of their investment. They understood the importance of shaping audience perception and leveraged their marketing skills and star power to achieve it. One of the key steps they took was to build a strong social media presence for the club. With over 850,000 followers across Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok, Wrexham FC has turned this social media boost into lucrative revenue opportunities.

The club has secured kit sponsorship deals with TikTok, Aviation American Gin, Expedia, and Vistaprint, and currently holds second place in the National League standings. But the impact of their investment goes beyond the club itself. The newfound attention on Wrexham FC has shone a spotlight on the picturesque Welsh town, attracting fans worldwide and boosting the local tourism economy.

The success of the pilot season of "Welcome to Wrexham" has led to the greenlighting of seasons 2 and 3. The docuseries is currently streaming on Disney+ Hotstar, further increasing the global reach of the club and the town.

In conclusion, the investment of Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney in Wrexham FC is a testament to the power of creative storytelling, marketing genius, and a visionary approach. Their efforts have not only revitalized a once-forgotten football club but also transformed the fortunes of an entire community. The success of their investment has given hope to struggling football clubs around the world and serves as an inspiration to anyone looking to make a positive impact on their community. Hopefully, it will also show other sports owners what happens when you invest in your team and listen to the fans.

With Wrexham’s recent promotion into League Two from the National League, the club is still three promotions away from the highly coveted Premier League. While a lofty dream, the whole world will continue to watch and root for this incredible underdog story. The investment of Reynolds and McElhenney in Wrexham FC is a true testament to what is possible with creativity, vision, and determination.
